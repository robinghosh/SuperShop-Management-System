FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions and Answers Listed Below:

Q: How to add products?
Answer: On Products page, click on the add icon and fill the details. The barcode is unique for each product.
Q: How to delete a product?
Answer: On Products page, in product list here is a delete icon in the Actions column for each product. Click it to remove a product.
Q: How to update/edit a products details?
Answer: On Products page, in product list, click edit icon to edit/update a products details.
Q: What if I forgot my password/username?
Answer: Contact system Admin, if you forgot your password/username.
